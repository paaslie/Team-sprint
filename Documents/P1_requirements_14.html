P1 Project Requirements

Petter, Sixten, Sara, Carina og Ine

22. september 2021

The client

  • Client name: Team Sprint
  • Contact persons: Arne Skuterud (Head coach of Team Sprint) & Carina Jagtøyen

Our client, Team Sprint, is a team of four Norwegian speed skaters. We were introduced to this client by Carina, who is one of the speed skaters in Team Sprint. As the name of the team implies, the four athletes specialize in the sprint distances of speed skating, which are 100, 500 and 1000 meters. Team Sprint has a close cooperation with the Norwegian Skating Federation and the Norwegian National Sprint Team. Their goal is to provide sprinters that find themselves on the second highest level with the opportunity to develop and climb the last few steps up to the highest level of sprint in Norway. Team Sprint depends on the support from sponsors and this website will be used as a resource for reaching out to potential sponsors and promoting their current sponsors. We will create a website called Team Sprint Skøyter.


Purpose and goals

Because of the Norwegian tradition of long-distance speed skating, Team Sprint seeks to educate and inspire Norwegian coaches and young speed skaters into wanting to specialize in sprint. This is also seen as the main purpose of this website. Our goal to fulfill the main purpose is to create a platform for Team Sprint to share their knowledge about sprint training. This will be done through publishing training programs, exercises, and thoughts about sprint technique. In other words, we need to provide users with the opportunity to watch videos of the exercises that are mentioned in the training programs. Another goal to fulfill such a purpose is to give a description of Team Sprint´s work and goals, a short introduction of each athlete, and provide updates from competitions and training camps. To fulfill the secondary purpose of reaching potential sponsors and promoting the current sponsors, our goal is to include a lot of photos and videos that show off sponsor products and logos, give a brief description of every sponsor, and provide Team Sprint´s contact information.


Audience

The main audience of this website will be coaches and athletes from different speed skating clubs and teams in Norway. This group will use the website to learn about sprint technique and to find inspiration from exercises that contribute to improve the attributes that are essential for sprint on ice. The audience also consists of those who are interested in following the journey and results of the Team Sprint athletes, such as speed skating enthusiasts, family members and friends of the Team Sprint athletes and sponsors.


Content of the website and Organization

  1. “About Us”

    This section will introduce Team Sprint to the users through a short description of their work and their goals. Additionally, each of the athletes and coaches will be presented with a portrait, a few words about themselves and a list of their personal records. This page will provide other speed skaters, coaches, supporters, and potential sponsors with the opportunity to get to know Team Sprint on a deeper level.

  2. "Sprint training"
    • Training programs
    • Exercises
    • Technique

    This section will include three subsections: the first one containing training programs, the second one containing videos of the exercises that are included in the training programs, and the last one containing thoughts and explanations regarding sprint technique. These three pages will help us fulfill the purpose of educating and inspiring Norwegian coaches and speed skaters and might therefore be seen as the most important pages of the website. This section may also be found very interesting for speed skating enthusiasts that want to learn more about sprint training. Team Sprint would like the “Exercises”-page to be organized in categories based on the training program they belong to.

  3. “Updates and Results”

    This section will include updates from Team Sprint´s training camps and competitions, such as information about their whereabouts, what kind of training they are doing, how things are going and results from the competitions they participate in. This page will contribute to fulfilling the goal of inspiring young speed skaters, as well as making it easier for supporters and sponsors to access information about Team Sprint´s performance progress.

  4. "Our Sponsors"

    This section will include the logos of Team Sprint’s sponsors with a following, brief description of each of the sponsors. This page will help us achieve the goal of promoting current sponsors of Team Sprint.

  5. “Instagram”

    This section will include embedded photos and videos from Team Sprint’s Instagram account and will be located on the front page of the website. This section will meet the needs of those who want to follow the everyday life and journey of the Team Sprint athletes.


Footnote: Every page of the website may include a footnote with Team Sprints contact information and links to their Facebook and Instagram account. We may also create a section below the contact information that will contain the logos of Team Sprints sponsors. This will help Team Sprint promote their sponsors furthermore and give potential sponsors the opportunity to contact Team Sprint directly.


Functional and Non-Functional Requirements

  1. The front page of the website should have a navigation bar that contains links to the following pages: “About Us”, “Sprint Training”, “Updates and Results” and “Our Sponsors”.

  2. When clicking on “Sprint Training” in the navigation bar, a dropdown menu containing the options “Training Programs”, “Exercises” and “Technique” should appear.

  3. Users should be allowed to click on the exercises included in the training programs and then be led to the corresponding videos of the exercises, located in the “Exercises”-page. The videos should open in new tabs so that the users don’t have to find their way back to the training program.

  4. All the pages of the website should have the same clean look, mainly using the colors #FFFFFF and #033EA7 (the colors of Team Sprint’s logo). This to ensure that the website appears professional and easy to navigate.

  5. The embedded Instagram photos and videos should include the caption.

  6. The logos of Team Sprint’s sponsors, sectioned in the footnote of every page, should function as links to the respective sponsors’ own websites. These websites should open in new tabs.

Final location

Team Sprint will donate the domain name, which eventually will be teamsprintskoyter.no.
Carina will be responsible for the maintenance of the website.
